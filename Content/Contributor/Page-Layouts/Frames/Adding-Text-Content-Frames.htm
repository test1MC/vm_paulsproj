Adding Text and Content to Frames

how to add text and content to a frame
  1. Click in the Frame Contents window pane and add text or other content (e.g., images), just as you would add content to a topic in the XML Editor.
 notes

In addition to adding regular text and other content, you can add page numbers, Chapter Number variables, Section Number variables, Volume Number variables, Heading variables, and Running Head variables to frames. See Inserting Page Numbers into Frames and Inserting Heading Variables Into Frames.
