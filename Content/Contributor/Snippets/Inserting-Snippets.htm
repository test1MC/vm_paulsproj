Inserting Snippets

Snippets are pre-set chunks of formatted content. You can insert them into a new document. They cannot be inserted into review topics or contribution documents.

how to insert a snippet
  1. Open the new document.
  2. Place your cursor where you want to insert the snippet.

    • Ribbon In the Single Source section select Snippet.

    • Local Toolbar In the local toolbar at the top of the XML Editor click .
    • Keyboard Shortcut Press CTRL+R.
  4. In the Open dialog, navigate to the snippet you want to insert and double-click it.
