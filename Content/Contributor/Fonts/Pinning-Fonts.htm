Pinning Fonts

How to Pin Fonts from the Font Properties Dialog

  3. Select the Font tab. From the Font field, select the font you want to pin. A pin appears to the right of the font. You can pin fonts from any font group.

  4. To pin the font to the font list, click . The pin changes to to show that the font has been pinned to the font list. Pinned fonts are also added to the Pinned Fonts group at the top of the font list.

  5. To unpin the font, click the pin again and it will be moved back to All Fonts group at the bottom of the list.

 How to Pin Fonts from the Font Family Field

  2. In the Font section click the down arrow in the Font Family field.

  3. Hover over the font you want to pin. A pin appears to the right of the font.

  4. To pin the font to the Font Family field, click . The pin changes to to show that the font has been pinned to the font list. Pinned fonts are also added to the Pinned Fonts group at the top of the Font Family field.

  5. To unpin the font, click the pin again and it will be moved back to All Fonts group at the bottom of the list.
