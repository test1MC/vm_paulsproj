Renewing a Central Subscription

How to Renew a Central Subscription

    • Home Page If you’ve added the Storage and Usage widget (see About Widgets), click to open the Home page. Then click Renew.

      OR

    • Settings Dialog At the top of Central, click your user name, then click Settings. Click the Server tab. Under the MadCap Central Expiration Date graph, click Renew.

    The Add to License dialog opens.

  2. In the Product field, select your MadCap Central license.

  3. In the Renew field, select whether you want your license to renew automatically or not. If you select Auto, your subscription will renew automatically every month or year. If you select None, you will xxx.

    The amount shown after “Total Due Today” does not include tax.

    You can also add more seats or storage by completing those fields.

  5. Click Continue. The Confirm Purchase dialog opens. A message shows you the number of user licenses and/or the amount of storage space you are purchasing, as well as the total amount of the purchase (exclusive of tax).
  6. Click Complete Purchase. Your card will be charged.
