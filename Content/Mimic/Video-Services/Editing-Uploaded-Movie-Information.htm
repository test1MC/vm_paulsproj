Editing Uploaded Movie Information

When you upload a video, you include details about that movie (e.g., title, tags, privacy level). After you have uploaded the movie, you can go back and edit the movie details at any time.

How to View Uploaded Videos

  1. In the Services section, click Video Services.

    The Video Services window pane opens.

  2. Log on to YouTube or Vimeo. See Logging In to a Video Service.

    If you have previously uploaded videos, they will appear in the Videos section.

  3. In the Videos section, right-click on a video and select Edit Basic Info.

    The Edit Video Information dialog opens.

  4. Make changes as needed, then click OK.
 What's Next?

After editing movie information, you can view the movie or remove it. See Viewing an Uploaded Movie and Removing an Uploaded Movie.
