 Notes

If you want to clean out files in order to save on storage space, you can delete files that are attached to individual tasks.

To do this, click on the task title to open the Task Details dialog. Then hover the attachment, click , and click OK in the confirmation dialog.

In addition to editing the details for a task, you can change its state by moving it from one milestone to another (e.g., from “To Do” to “In Progress”). See Moving Tasks.
