Editing Teams

How to edit a Team
  1. Click . The Teams page opens.
  2. Find the team you want to edit in the grid, and click in an empty space so that the entire row is highlighted.
  3. In the toolbar click . The Edit Team Profile dialog opens.

  4. You can make changes to any of the team information (name, avatar image, description, projects/users associated with the team).

  5. Click Save and Finish Editing.
