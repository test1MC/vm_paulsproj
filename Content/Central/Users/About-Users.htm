About Users

Introduction

See Users Page.

Users can work independently, or they can work in collaboratively, sending messages to one another, assigning tasks, etc. If you have many users, you can organize them into teams (see About Teams).

Some users might even be added to more than one key. For example, you might be an independent contractor who does work for a couple of companies, each of which has a MadCap Central license. When you log in to Central, you can switch from one company to the other simply by choosing the appropriate key at the top of the interface.
